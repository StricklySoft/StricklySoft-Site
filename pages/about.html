About StricklySoft

Purpose-built software designed for clarity, efficiency, and results.

Who We Are

StricklySoft is a North Carolina–based software company founded by senior developer Mark Strickland. With decades of enterprise software experience, Mark launched StricklySoft to provide focused, efficient, and professional-grade SaaS tools for businesses who value clarity and performance.


Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify complex workflows through clean, effective software — reducing friction, increasing productivity, and delivering real business value. We believe great software should be intuitive, scalable, and secure from the ground up.


What We Do

  • Design and build powerful SaaS applications for small and midsize businesses
  • Engineer backend services in ASP.NET Core with a focus on performance and security
  • Craft clean and consistent UI experiences using React and Next.js
  • Deliver systems that scale — backed by SQL Server, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB

Our Values

  • Efficiency: Code should do its job without getting in the way
  • Security: We follow best practices by default, not as an afterthought
  • Clarity: UI and UX should be clean, consistent, and accessible
  • Integrity: We build long-term relationships through honesty and reliability
